How It Works

1. Write & set a PIN. Message encrypted with AES-256-GCM. PBKDF2 derives the key from your PIN — it's never stored.

2. QR carries everything. Salt, IV, ciphertext, and expiry are all encoded in the QR. Zero server involvement.

3. Any device can scan. iPhone, Android, laptop — anyone with DropNote can scan and decrypt with the PIN.

4. Expires automatically. After the timer, the QR is rejected — no one can read it even with the correct PIN.
